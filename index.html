Research Interests

I focus on advancing the field of natural language processing with particular emphasis on:

  • Developing commonsense reasoning models to enhance natural language understanding.
  • Improving pre-trained language models by integrating commonsense knowledge.
  • Exploring and designing multimodal reasoning frameworks for better contextual understanding.

Projects

Commonsense Reasoning in NLP

Developed a novel framework for integrating commonsense reasoning into transformer-based language models, enhancing downstream tasks like question answering and text generation.

Pre-trained Language Model Enhancement

Worked on augmenting BERT-like models with structured commonsense knowledge bases, improving their performance on zero-shot reasoning benchmarks.

Multimodal Reasoning

Designed a hybrid model combining vision and language inputs for cross-modal commonsense reasoning tasks, including image captioning and scene understanding.
